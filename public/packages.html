Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Kerala wedding coverage packages, billing, and timelines.

How does the ₹5,000/- advance booking work?

To lock in your wedding date, we only require a low-friction advance of ₹5,000/- via Google Pay. Upon payment, we send your professional Digital Invoice and immediately build a dedicated WhatsApp planning group containing you, your editors, and photographers.

Are travel expenses and stay/accommodation included?

Travel and stay/accommodation expenses are strictly excluded/not covered in all packages, regardless of the location. Actual travel expenses (fuel/conveyance) and stay/accommodation (if required due to early morning starts or multi-day coverage) must be provided or covered by the client for the photography crew.

Can we select our own background songs for Reels and Highlights?

Absolutely! We highly encourage couples to select their preferred music tracks. During our video editing phase in your dedicated WhatsApp group, you can share audio links, and our editors will sync the visual highlights to your favorite tunes.

How long does the raw photo delivery and album sorting take?

We deliver full raw photos via high-speed Google Drive folders within 24 to 48 hours post-event. You then select up to 150 of your favorite images for the printed album, which is compiled, bound, and couriered straight to your address.

Who covers the couple photoshoot resort/entry fees?

Our packages cover all photography and videography talent, visual gear, and editing. Any external location entry fees, resort permissions, or special destination charges (e.g. resort permission fees at Pura Vida or Kuthira Malika) are paid directly by the client at the venue.
